About Us

SATVA was established in 2018. Satva NGO is an Indian Organization,impacting the lives of over 20 thousand children and their families ever ear. We have more than 150 projects on education, healthcare,livelihood,and youth empowerment in over 500 remote villages and urban slums.

Vision

Work as a catalst in bringing sustainable change in the lives of lesser privileged children and families with a life ccle approach of development.
Enable the civil societ across the India to engage proactivel in the change process through the philosophy of civic driven change.

Mission

Satva is to empower mentally disabled children, innovative healthcare and livelihoodprogrammes.
To sensitize disabled children, youth in general to promote Civic Driven Change.

How we Work

While working in remote rural areas, we realized that an intensive on ground intervention is needed, which might be beyond the capacit of communit based organization and hence, we directly implement such projects.
